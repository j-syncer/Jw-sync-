Preparing your workspace…

Merge JW Library backups privately in your browser

Merge, browse, and edit your JW Library notes, highlights, and bookmarks — entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Launch App →

Local-only processing

All merging happens in your browser via WebAssembly. No server. No uploads. Complete privacy.

Tags & highlights merged

Intelligently combine tags, highlights, bookmarks and margin notes across all your backups.

Date-specific extraction

Extract content from a specific backup date to restore exactly what you had at any point in time.

Browse & edit notes

Search your entire library, edit notes, manage tags, change highlight colours, then download the updated backup.

Review before you download

See exactly which notes were edited on more than one device and choose which version wins — full transparency, no black box.

JW Sync processes all data locally — your files never leave your device.
